1. What are your credentials?

Gabriela graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a Management Information Systems degree. She has specialized continuing her studies following the UI/UX Specialization in Coursera, where she has finished the Visual Elements of User Interface Design from California Institute of Fine Arts.

“This design-centric course examines the broad question of what an interface is and what role a designer plays in creating a user interface. Learning how to design and articulate meaning using color, type, and imagery is essential to making interfaces function clearly and seamlessly.” - Coursera

2. Do I need a website?

Depending on the audience you’re serving/reaching, you may not need one. Some communities can be reached solely through social media, which is so prevalent today. You can see different social media presets Gabriela has done for othre clients here. Gabriela will communicate if this is the case for you.

Disclaimer: Gabriela is NOT liable to any "loss of business" or guarantees any profit to your business due to having or designing your website.

3. Will you work on my website full-time?

Gabriela values time and will be clear on the time commitment she can make on your project. She has experience knowing how much work she can do during a week so and she will not overcommit to more that she can handle.

4. Do you charge hourly or project-based?

Project-based. The payments will be distributed to before, during, and after the project is finished. The amount and times will be set before starting to work together.

5. How do you communicate during a website-project?

Gabriela will commit to once a week phone calls to ask any questions she has and also update on the status of the work.

6. Can I get a quote for my project?

Yes! Please email a summary of your project, leave us a link to your site or social media, and some dates/times you’re available for Gabriela to call you to talk about your project. Then, she will get back to you with a quote for your project.
