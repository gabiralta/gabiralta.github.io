Gabriela fell in love with design

while sailing the ocean blue for four years while traveling and working with locals in Africa, the Caribbean, and the Americas. With a background in business, her recent work in various global campaigns has brought her passion to life: to understand and capture the heart of your business into a simple, yet powerful design.

She graduated from the McCombs School of Business at the University of Texas at Austin after finishing her degree in Management Information Systems with a minor in French.

She has lived in 7 countries in different continents and has helped her to have different perspective on life. Gabriela speaks 3.5 languages, and is currently learning Russian.
